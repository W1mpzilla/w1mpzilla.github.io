Living Life


I'm born in Genoa, the capital of the Liguria, a northern Italian region facing the Mediterranean Sea.
I lived in a town not far from Genoa, in a locality called Italian Riviera, known for its small villages like Le Cinque Terre.
When still very young, a career opportunity made my parents move to the French country side, in the Isere region, where i grew up.
After getting my scientific high school graduation diploma, i followed my parents and moved back to my Italian home town.
From here i enrolled the Biological Sciences degree course at the Genoa University, were i got my degree and master degree.
Upon graduating, i was granted an internship at the Italian Institute of Technology in Genoa, where i worked on a neurobiology project.
The paper published, i started looking for enrolling a PhD course, successfully winning a PhD scholarship in Marseille.
I worked one year at the Immunology Center of Marseille-Luminy, before leaving the PhD course due to issues with the project.

After being decieved by my PhD experiance and project, i decided to open up to other fields related to my personal interest.
I initially found an occupation as park keeper, alongside i started learning on my own electronic and software development.
While working, i got in touch with the AMD support team, applying as voluntary for AMD graphics drivers beta-tester program.
I spent the next eight month after my park keeper occupation as voluntary beta-tester, troubleshooting and improving graphic drivers.
Following this nice experience, i had to come back to a compensated position, i began looking for a new job as IT support.
I got hired by a Montpelier company as roaming IT technician, for the Marseille-Aix-Orange region providing hardware support.
Moving onward due to a growing numbers of corporations in the process of digital transform, i switched to OS deployment.
I migrated to Windows 10 several Crédit Agricole offices in Marseille, before switching to the Marignane Airbus Helicopters site.
After spending a year at Airbus between OS migration and broad IT support tasks, i got transferred to the AP-HM hospital.
I worked at the Assistance Publique - Hôpitaux de Marseille a couple of months, until the COVID19 spread.
At this point, having learnt the basics of software development by myself, i enrolled to a Symfony PHP MySQL programming training course.
